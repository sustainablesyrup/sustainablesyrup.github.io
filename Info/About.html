Linearity is a shallow water — sometimes exposed at low tide, sometimes submerged when the tide rises. 
Welcome to Wang Ruoxi’s website! She withdraws into this constrained medium, attempting here to sketch a terrain of shallow waters.
Wang Ruoxi is an artist and writer attuned to endurance and movement through space.
Born in 2000 and raised in Nanjing, she received her MFA in Art from the University of California, Riverside, and her BA in Anthropology and Studio Art from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities.
